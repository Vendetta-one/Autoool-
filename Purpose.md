<h3>📍 The Problem</h3>
<p>
  Our university is located in a hilly terrain where the nearest transport hub, <b>Wakhnaghat</b>, is 3km away. 
  With a fixed auto fare of <b>₹100</b>, traveling alone is expensive. Students prefer to split the fare 
  (₹33 per person), but finding two other people heading out at the exact same time can be a hassle.
</p>

<h3>🚀 The Solution</h3>
<p>
  <b>Autool</b> is a real-time pooling platform designed to bridge this gap. It allows students to see:
</p>
<ul>
  <li>How many students are currently waiting at the main gate.</li>
  <li>Real-time availability of autos.</li>
  <li>Instant "Join Pool" requests to ensure a full ride of 3 people every time.</li>
</ul>

<h3>✨ Key Features</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Live Counter:</b> Real-time updates on waiting passengers.</li>
  <li><b>Fare Calculator:</b> Automatic split-cost visualization (₹100 → ₹33).</li>
  <li><b>Authentication:</b> Secure login to ensure only university students use the service.</li>
</ul>
